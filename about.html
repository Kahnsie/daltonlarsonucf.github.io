Our Story

Our goal at Lawn and Order is to teach the average person how to garden using their backyard. Want to start growing fruits and vegetables to save money on groceries? Check! Want that amazing garden you can wake up to each morning? Check! Tired of having your yard look empty and gloomy? We got you covered! Anybody can garden with the right tools and instructions. Bringing it to your computer/mobile devices makes everything easier.
